About Me

I am Pranjal Vyas, a clinical psychologist with a passion for bridging modern psychology and ancient spiritual techniques. As a person with spastic diplegic cerebral palsy, my journey has been one of resilience and constant learning. My work integrates mental health expertise with a deep understanding of spiritual healing.
