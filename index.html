About Me

I am Pranjal Vyas, a psychologist with a passion for integrating modern psychology with ancient spiritual techniques. As someone living with spastic diplegic cerebral palsy, my journey has been one of resilience and learning. My work emphasizes mental health and the importance of holistic healing.
