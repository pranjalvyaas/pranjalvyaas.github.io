Welcome to My World

I am Pranjal Vyas, a psychologist and advocate for inclusivity, living with Spastic Diplegic Cerebral Palsy. My journey has been about transforming challenges into opportunities, blending modern psychology with Indian spirituality for holistic healing.

My Philosophy

Combining the principles of modern psychology with the timeless wisdom of Indian scriptures like the Vedas and Puranas, I aim to create holistic healing solutions. This approach integrates emotional well-being with spiritual grounding, offering individuals a unique pathway to self-discovery and mental peace.
